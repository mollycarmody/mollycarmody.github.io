Ultrahaptic Technology

Ultrahaptic Technology uses ultrasound to create 3D shapes and textures that are felt but not seen

Array of ultrasonic transducers and signal processing algorithms create focal points of ultrasonic energy on palms or fingers
When the ultrasound is focused onto the surface of the skin, it induces a shear wave in skin tissue
